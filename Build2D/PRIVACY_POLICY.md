Privacy Policy for Room Planner

Last updated: 10 February 2026

Overview

Room Planner respects your privacy and is committed to handling your information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we safeguard it when you use the Room Planner application.

By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

User Information

Depending on how you access the application, we may collect:
- Your email address during login or registration
- Basic profile details such as name and profile image when using Google Sign-In
- Secure authentication credentials required to maintain your session

Application Usage Information

To ensure smooth functionality and improvement of features, we may collect:
- Details about how the application is used
- Floor plans and room layouts created within the app
- Basic technical details related to device and network usage

Google Account Data

If you choose to sign in using Google, we may receive:
- Your registered Google email address
- Your public Google profile name
- Your profile image, if available

This information is used strictly for authentication purposes.

How Your Information Is Used

We use collected data to:
- Operate and maintain the Room Planner application
- Verify user identity and manage login sessions
- Save, retrieve, and manage room plans
- Notify users about important updates or changes
- Improve performance, usability, and reliability

Data Protection and Security

Security Practices

We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure login and authentication mechanisms
- Restricted access to sensitive information
- Periodic security checks

Data Storage

- Room plans are stored securely
- Authentication data is handled using trusted security standards
- Industry-accepted best practices are followed for data protection

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- View the personal data associated with your account
- Request corrections to incorrect information
- Ask for deletion of your account and related data
- Export your saved room plans
- Opt out of non-essential communications

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.
